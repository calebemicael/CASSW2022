Young Professionals Workshop


3rd CASSW Young Professionals Workshop



Language: Portuguese

We are pleased to announce that the 3rd CASSW Young Professionals' theme is Advice for a Successful Career. The objective is to point out what other skills beyond technical knowledge are fundamental for young professionals to develop in order to achieve excellent results in their career. This year the event will be held online on 1st October 2021 from 1:30 p.m to 4:00 p.m. The event starts with some valuable CV elaboration considerations, followed by a roundtable discussion where Gustavo Wilke (Google) talks with professionals from world-leading technology and microelectronics companies about their experiences.

This event will cover emerging topics including home-office after covid-19, new job requirements for the future, how to develop soft-skills, and much more.

Invited speakers:

  • Gustavo Wilke - Google

  • Gracieli Posser - Cadence

  • Guilherme Flach - Synopsys

  • Arthur Liraneto - CESAR
