Call for Posters


INSTRUCTIONS

The posters can cover topics on Circuits and Systems including, but not limited to:

  -   Analog and Digital Circuits
  -   Machine Learning Circuits
  -   Mixed-Mode Circuits
  -   Biomedical Electronics
  -   Communication/RF Circuits
  -   Wireless Sensor Networks
  -   Nanoelectronics
  -   Electronic Design Automation
  -   Video Processing Architectures
  -   VLSI Systems and Applications
  -   Low Power Design
  -   FPGA Design and Applications
  -   Circuit Testing
  -   High-Level and Logic Synthesis
  -   Fault-Tolerant Circuits
  -   Approximate Computing




A limited number of IEEE + CAS student 2022 subscriptions will be awarded for the first poster submissions (i.e., first authors who submitted and presented their posters). The students awarded must be affiliated with the CASS-RS Chapter.


SUBMISSION FORMAT


Posters must be electronically submitted using an A4 landscape layout (WxH = 29.7 cm x 21 cm) in PDF format, along with a 500-word abstract. The submission is available on the event's webpage in EasyChair - click here. After examination by experts of the community, the authors of selected works will be asked to produce a 5-min video describing their work. After each poster session, the authors are invited to participate in a Q&A session.

There is not a mandatory template, but authors would preferably use the following poster template for their convenience.

      -   Honor Mention - USD 100,00
      -   Honor Mention - USD 100,00

  -   Best Poster Award - USD 200,00
  -   Honor Mention - USD 100,00
  -   Honor Mention - USD 100,00
