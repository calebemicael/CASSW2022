Hybrid Edition, Brazil
November 3-4, 2022


About

The 12th IEEE Circuits and Systems Workshop will take place in Porto Alegre from November 2 to 3, 2022. While we encourage all attendees to be in person experience, the Workshop is also going to be presented virtually through the IEEE CASS Rio Grande do Sul Chapter YouTube channel . It is an event intended for academic and industrial exchange between local and foreign researchers. The speakers are renowned researchers from institutions with significant work in the field of circuits and systems. The event will last for two days and the program consists of a series of invited talks and poster sessions. The event will be co-located with the 4th IEEE CASS-RS Young Professionals Workshop.

Program

The program will cover topics on Circuits and Systems including, but not limited to:

  -   Analog and Digital Circuits
  -   Machine Learning Circuits
  -   Mixed-Mode Circuits
  -   Biomedical Electronics
  -   Communication/RF Circuits
  -   Wireless Sensor Networks
  -   Nanoelectronics
  -   Electronic Design Automation
  -   Video Processing Architectures
  -   VLSI Systems and Applications
  -   Low Power Design
  -   FPGA Design and Applications
  -   Circuit Testing
  -   High-Level and Logic Synthesis
  -   Fault-Tolerant Circuits
  -   Approximate Computing
