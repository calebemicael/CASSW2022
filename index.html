About

The 12th IEEE Circuits and Systems Workshop will take place in Porto Alegre from November 2 to 3, 2022. While we encourage all attendees to be an in-person experience, the Workshop is also going to be presented virtually through the IEEE CASS Rio Grande do Sul Chapter YouTube channel. It is an event intended for academic and industrial exchange between local and foreign researchers. The speakers are renowned researchers from institutions with significant work in the field of circuits and systems. The event will last for two days and the program consists of a series of invited talks and poster sessions. The event will be co-located with the 4th IEEE CASS-RS Young Professionals Workshop.
